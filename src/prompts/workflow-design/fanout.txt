<workflow_guidance topic="fanout">
<purpose>Use parallelism without launching unbounded agents or duplicating work.</purpose>
<rules>
<rule>Select and cap inputs before fan-out; never map over an unbounded project tree.</rule>
<rule>Use parallel(items, worker, { label }) only when items are independent.</rule>
<rule>Use pipeline(items, stages) when every item follows the same ordered stages.</rule>
<rule>mapreduce applies its extensions/tools selection to input selection, map workers, and reduction; verifier applies its selection to voters and reduction.</rule>
<rule>Record skipped scope with log(...) or trace(...) so users can see what was intentionally left out.</rule>
<rule>Workflow runs obey the project maxParallelAgents cap; design fan-outs as bounded queues, not bursts.</rule>
</rules>
</workflow_guidance>
